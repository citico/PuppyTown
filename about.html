Our Story

As our first litter of puppies were climbing all over our son, he exclaimed, "Welcome to Puppytown!" The name stuck. This is our story.

It brings us joy every time we look out the window and see our children running and playing with our amazing dogs. Golden retrievers have been a wonderful addition to our family and are great with our kids. Goldens are loyal, sweet natured, loving pets. It is our desire to provide other families with these marvelous and sweet companions.

We do everything we can to ensure that our puppies are healthy, happy, and put in loving homes. We cuddle and play with the puppies daily to ensure that they love human attention. They are all vet checked and are kept up to date on vaccines to give you the peace of mind that your puppy is healthy. In addition, all of our dogs are AKC certified.

Golden retrievers are a great addition to any family. Contact us today to find out if we have a puppy that will be a fit with your family.
